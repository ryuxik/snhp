# PRD 3: Enterprise B2B Layer (The Sales Shield)

## 1. The Strategy (Elad Gil / B2B SaaS)
The deepest pockets in AI belong to companies building Autonomous Sales Agents. But their SDRs are crippled by a lack of negotiation leverage and contextual elasticity. Our product acts as a rigorous **Constraint Injector** deployed directly into their prompt chains, mathematically shielding their margins from LLM sycophancy.

## 2. The Jobsian Vision (Momentum over Math)
> *"Enterprise software is famously bloated. VPs of Sales don't buy math—they buy momentum and revenue. We don't sell 'features', we sell a shield that optimizes margin without killing the deal. The dashboard for the VP of Sales should have exactly one massive number: 'Margin Recovered by SNHP.' Give them complete control over 'Quota Elasticity,' but make the interface pristine. We sell executive peace of mind."*

## 3. Product Requirements

### 3.1 The "Injected Constraint" Architecture
SNHP functions as a Pre-Generation API, completely abandoning high-latency interception.
- **Before** the enterprise LLM drafts an email to a prospect, it queries SNHP with the CRM context.
- SNHP runs the Bayesian math and passes the exact **Nash Equilibrium Offer Boundaries** directly into the system prompt of the executing AI agent, guaranteeing a safe, mathematically optimized generation on the first pass.

### 3.2 Contextual Quota Elasticity (Glass Box)
Sales Orgs operate on quarterly deadlines.
- The dashboard allows CROs and VPs to tune the "Quota Elasticity" slider. At the end of Q3, they can dramatically loosen the mathematical constraints to prioritize closure volume over surplus margins. 
- We extract contextual parameters (Urgency, Elasticity, Pipeline Strength) and present them transparently. Sales leaders can override these parameters natively before the engine runs.

### 3.3 The Executive Dashboard
A beautiful, minimalist web dashboard for VPs of Sales.
- **The Synergy Log**: A feed of enterprise negotiations and exactly how SNHP anchored the counter-offer based on context.
- **Margin Recovered**: A rolling ticker of the exact dollar amount saved by holding the line at the SNHP optimal anchor vs the client's initial bid.

### 3.4 SSO and SOC2 Compliance
To sell to Fortune 500 procurement teams, the API must support Google/Okta Single Sign-On. Strict data minimization ensures context is ingested but never retained beyond the memory span of the active CRM deal.

## 4. Key Success Metric
**Margin Delta Saved**: Focus on landing 'Whales' (large AI SDR companies). We track the exact delta recovered per deal and visualize the ROI dynamically on their monthly invoice to guarantee renewal.
