Capture rate: 138%. SNHP-seller's gain (0.075) exceeds the counterparty's loss (0.054) by 0.021 — that's value created, not transferred. Without SNHP, vanilla LLMs settle for sub-Pareto bundles; SNHP's outcome picker finds bundles with strictly more joint utility, then captures more of the larger pie. 10 of 20 seeds show SNHP gain AND joint surplus growth simultaneously. See seed-by-seed →

Honest caveat — direction matters. The positive-sum story is specific to SV (SNHP-seller × vanilla-buyer). On the buy side (SNHP-buyer × vanilla-seller), SNHP is purely extractive and joint surplus drops by ~0.04. Across all 4 of our tuning runs, averaged joint welfare in asymm pairings is consistently negative (−0.01 to −0.05). Sell-side adoption is the clean win; buy-side ships with a cryptographic commit-reveal mechanism designed to convert the buyer from second-mover to binding-first-mover. Documented at /for-builders.

Statistical honesty footnote.

Every number on this page comes from N=20 paired-seed LLM tournaments. That's enough seeds for some claims and not enough for others. Power analysis on the pooled SD across our 4 tuning runs:

Claim Effect/SD N for p<0.01 Status at N=20
H3 head-to-head margin (+12%) 0.79 19 replicated (E1: p<0.0001 on disjoint N=20)
H2_A single SNHP-seller benefit (+7.5%) 0.66 27 significant (p=0.006)
H1 cooperation premium (+7%) 0.50 47 confirmed (E2: p=0.00047 at N=50)
H2_B single SNHP-buyer benefit (+3.5%) 0.22 238 demoted (would need ~$24 to confirm; not on hero)

Both hero claims are now empirically validated across multiple disjoint runs at p<0.001. H3 replicated on seeds 2000-2019 (E1, +0.150 vs T1's +0.121, both p<0.0001). H1 upgraded from "borderline at N=20 (p=0.058)" to "confirmed at N=50 (p=0.00047)" via the planned power-up.

Mechanism research dead-end: we tested whether a cryptographic commit-reveal mechanism (BATNA + preference reveal) could push past the 97% Pareto plateau. Sim predicted +8% lift; LLM tournament showed −0.6% (p=0.98 wrong direction). Reproduces a previously-observed pattern where the no-LLM simulator inflates mechanism wins by 13× over actual LLM behavior. Mechanism roadmap shelved until LLM-loop tuning is feasible at scale.

The framework that produced these numbers (param tuning + faithful sim + LLM-loop validation) is documented in CHANGELOG.md.
