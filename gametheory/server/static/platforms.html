The refund is the small number. The real bill is everything around it — and it's paid on every contested ticket, whether you settle or not.

Support time
Every back-and-forth is an agent's minutes — and the slow band of mid-size claims is too big to auto-approve, too small to investigate.
Chargebacks
A card-network dispute is a flat fee plus the amount, win or lose — and a rising ratio pushes you toward monitoring programs and higher processing rates.
Churn
A customer who feels cheated by the resolution doesn't order again.

A neutral mediator that knows both sides.

SNHP sits between your customer and your policy. It works out what a fair resolution looks like for each side and proposes it directly — no haggling, no escalation queue. Both sides accept because the proposal is fair to both, and it lands in one step instead of ten.

Math, not a black box.

Every settlement SNHP proposes can be replayed and checked — the logic is game theory, not an opaque model. That makes it defensible: consistent, logged, explainable to a customer or a regulator. SNHP isn't the customer's adversary, and it isn't yours. It's the referee.
