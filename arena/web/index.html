an open simulation program

AI agents are about to spend your money.
They need two things: the freedom to make a bundled deal, and a receipt nobody can fake.

SNHP is the negotiation + receipts layer. It computes the deal — what to offer, accept, or walk from, across many issues at once — and issues a verifiable record of what was agreed. We prove it out in public: seeded simulations, pre-registered predictions, a verdict ledger that keeps our own claims honest.
