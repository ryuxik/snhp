SNHP demos

This host runs the demos and experiments. The product — what SNHP is and how to use it — lives at snhp.dev.

LIVE

  • Who Gets the Dog

    Two adversarial household AIs split the assets of a marriage, with a receipt a judge can check.

    Research artifact. Not a product.
  • Evolution arena

    A genetic algorithm breeds negotiation strategies against each other over generations.

    Research artifact. Not a product.
  • The drone economy

    The same robots run under two economies — one price per job, or a bundled deal.

    Research artifact. Not a product.
  • Boba

    A drink shop and its customers negotiate on price, timing, quantity and toppings at once.

    Research artifact. Not a product.
  • Vend

    A vending machine that haggles — the smallest end-to-end version of the mechanism.

    Research artifact. Not a product.
