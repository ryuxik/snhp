We publish the results that make us look bad.

SNHP is a claim about mechanism, so we hold it to a claim's discipline. Every prediction is written down before the run, with the condition that would kill it. The verdict is recorded either way — the wins and the nulls sit in the same ledger.

The method

Pre-registered predictions. Before a column runs, we state what we expect and why. No moving the goalposts after the numbers land.

Kill conditions. Each prediction ships with the observation that would falsify it. When one fires, the hypothesis is dead — and several have been.

A public verdict ledger. Every run's verdict is committed to the repo, pass or null. The nulls are load-bearing: they are how we know the mechanism, not the story, is what's carrying the weight.
