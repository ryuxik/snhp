Three exhibits. One engine, moving.

Every exhibit is the same shipped SNHP engine under a different skin. Nothing here is a mock-up: each is a live or replayed run you can drive, and each illustrates one measured result.

Exhibit 1 · live

The drone economy — same robots, different economics

The identical population runs two economies side by side: one price at a time, versus bundled multi-issue deals.

What it illustrates: logrolling grows the pie. When agents can trade across issues instead of splitting each one down the middle, a quarter to two-thirds of deals become bundles — and the joint outcome the engine reaches is the measured win, not a bigger grab by either side.
