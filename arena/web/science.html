what the science says — including against us
  • Real: on price, an evolved champion beats the raw SNHP recommender's own play by +27% on a held-out panel — a strategy evolution discovered.
  • Real: verified peers who cooperate both do better (+2% joint welfare at the shipped dial) — the payoff is joint and attestation-gated.
  • Not real, said so: the eye-catching “+119% on multi-issue” is a preference artifact — on the fair metric the raw logroller is already at the efficiency ceiling (88% vs 85%).
  • Not real, said so: mean fitness does not rise — a Red Queen treadmill; negotiated crossover does not beat uniform.
  • Every number reproduces: python -m arena.science --all
