# Registrar triage — four founder ideas (2026-07-17)

Scored against the house standard: genuine structural opposition (acceptance =
utility rule, never LLM disposition), a control arm, a bidirectional kill
registered before build, and no "snhp finds deals LLMs can't" claims — the
honest edge is protocol + privacy + verification. Benchmark: the divorce demo
(divorce/SPEC.md), which already demonstrates deadlock→bundle→receipt with a
passed 4-kill harness. A new demo must prove something it doesn't.

**Ranking: influencer wedge > brain-rot > scheduler > matchmaker.**

## 1. Schedule activity negotiator — TOY

Single-issue as pitched (one categorical slot): nothing to logroll. Inflating
the issue space (weekends × venue × who-pays × duration) just re-derives the
divorce demo's claim at 1/100th the stakes. No genuine opposition — both
parties want the hangout, the ZOPA is enormous, any procedure clears; often
the "middle ground" is a third option both like, i.e. recommendation/
taste-matching wearing negotiation clothes. Privacy/receipt pillars carry
zero load (nobody needs a receipt that you picked brunch). This is "The
Amicable" preset as an entire product — the spec already killed it.
Confound at maximum strength: giant-ZOPA agreeableness makes success
indistinguishable from politeness.

## 2. Chinese-style matchmaker — DEAD

Wrong mechanism class: a **matching market** (search/screening/deferred-
acceptance), not bilateral bargaining — no surplus division, no frontier;
each side applies a unilateral threshold. Nothing in gametheory/negotiation
addresses it; building it is net-new invention plus a two-sided marketplace
cold-start. The adversarial element that does exist (misrepresentation) is a
credential-verification problem, not what the notary attests. Outcome quality
is unverifiable at demo timescale (eloquence faking). Where real bargaining
exists in the domain (caili, apartment, hukou) it's a cultural/PR minefield.
Salvageable kernel for the drawer: "sealed dealbreaker intersection" —
both sides disclose sensitive criteria to a mediator that outputs only
MATCH/NO-MATCH with a nobody-peeked receipt (disclosure-digest DNA, but it's
private-set-intersection, a new protocol module, and still faces liquidity).

## 3. Influencer sponsorship negotiator — PRODUCT-WEDGE CANDIDATE (the real one)

The only idea mapping near-1:1 onto shipped surface, and it hits all three
pillars: sponsorships are textbook multi-issue (rate, deliverables, usage
rights, exclusivity, whitelisting, timeline, payment terms — isomorphic to
bundle.py's own SaaS example); the creator's mandate is set by offered
choices ("$4k with 90-day exclusivity, or $3k without?") — self-selection,
exactly what the WTP-null licenses, on the preflearn machinery; and the
receipt has a real principal-agent job (prove to the creator the agent stayed
inside the mandate; prove to the brand the counter came from a sealed
mandate). GTM adjacency: a brand negotiating many creators IS procurement of
media — same buyer species as the notary GTM.

Honest split: "auto-pipelined to better terms before reviewing" drifts toward
one-party optimization; the triage layer is portfolio scoring, not
negotiation. In any harness the brand must be a utility-rule agent (budget,
target CPM, walk-away = next-best creator), never a cooperative LLM.

Controls: (a) accept-as-offered, (b) flat rule-based counter (+X%, decline
exclusivity — "the email template"), (c) full-disclosure raw-LLM negotiation
(ARM-D analogue). Kills: K-down if median creator-surplus lift over the flat
counter < 15% (the product is an email template); K-up if engine deal-death
rate (brand walks on positive-joint-surplus deals) exceeds baseline by > 5pp.
Confound: anchoring — asking beats not asking, hence the flat-counter control
is mandatory.

Not a demo ("15% better terms" is a dashboard, not a gasp) — a wedge: the
only idea with a buyer holding money (talent managers charge 10–20% for this
exact labor), repeated pain (inbound deal flow), and net-new work that is
ingestion plumbing rather than mechanism invention.

**The one day to spend after the divorce demo ships goes here** — and per the
standing pipeline discipline (research frozen till buyer conversations), the
day is: a one-page harness spec (registered brand population, flat-counter
control, K-down 15% / K-up 5pp) + outreach to 3–5 talent managers asking what
they'd pay for auto-triage + mandate-bounded countering with a receipt.

## 4. Brain-rot negotiator — TOY (one salvageable variant)

One principal: the phone is your own past self's commitment device (Ulysses
contract — the Beeminder/Opal category), not an opposed party. "Win the
argument" = an LLM judge scoring excuse quality — the cooperative-LLM
confound promoted to being the entire product; acceptance-by-utility-rule is
impossible because the human side always defects (that's why the category
exists). Goodhart trap with teeth: gamified gatekeeping trains users to
prompt-inject their own guardrail, and every viral clip is "look how gullible
the judge is" — anti-branding for a company selling a skeptic that trusts
neither. Salvage: **parent-child screen-time negotiation** — two genuinely
opposed principals, a real bundle space (minutes × chores × homework ×
bedtime), a receipt with a job ("room-cleaned = 45 minutes; no
relitigating"). The divorce machinery at family stakes; a possible future
preset in the same product family. The phone-vs-you version cannot be
repaired.
